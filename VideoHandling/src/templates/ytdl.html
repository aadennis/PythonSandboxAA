Downloader for Youtube videos

This is just a wrapper to yt-dlp(.exe). See here for details of yt-dlp.

Click for Help
  • Radio buttons: is it a single video or a list of videos? Default: single video
  • "identifier of video or list"
    • Single video: From the YouTube URL after the video, enter the string after "v=".
    • List (of videos): From the YouTube URL, enter the string after "list=".
  • "sub_folder if required"
      The output video(s) is saved under the folder [data] in the app location on the server. Enter a value here if you want to categorize. For Example, "JumpRope" will create a folder [data/JumpRope]. For now, spaces in sub_folders are not supported. So, do not enter "Jump Rope", for example.
  • Save: the video is saved to the data folder or a sub-folder of data. See above.
