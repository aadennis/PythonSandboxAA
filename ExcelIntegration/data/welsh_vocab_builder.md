| Your Term | Duolingo Variant | Notes                                                                                                                                                |
| --------- | ---------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| flinedig  | wedi blino       | "flinedig" is an adjective; "wedi blino" is a verb phrase meaning "have tired". Both translate as "tired" but come from different grammatical roots. |
